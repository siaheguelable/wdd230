Abidjan Chamber of Commerce.

The purpose of the Abidjan Chamber of Commerce is to support and promote business growth,
entrepreneurship, and economic development in Abidjan. We provide a platform for local and international
businesses to connect, access resources, and navigate the commercial landscape. Through networking
events, training programs, and advocacy, we aim to foster innovation, facilitate trade, and create opportunities
that contribute to the city's economic prosperity.
