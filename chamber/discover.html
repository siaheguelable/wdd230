Documentary checks on import

Documentary checks on import The Ruling Center is a new concept that is part of the Ivorian government's strategy to strengthen the role of customs in the control of goods by replacing the Import Verification Programme for goods with new procedures for analysing, verifying and determining the value and classification of goods at destination. The Ruling Centre, managed in close collaboration with Ivorian customs, is responsible for the documentary control of imported goods, in accordance with the international principles of value calculation (WTO) and the harmonised system (WCO), through the use of tools based on cutting-edge technology.
